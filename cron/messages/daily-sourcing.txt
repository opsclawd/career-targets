Run the daily Calgary software careers discovery sweep in this workspace.

Read AGENTS.md and follow it exactly.

   - Find companies on local job boards (Calgary Economic Development, Startup Calgary, etc.)
   - Check directories and lists of Calgary tech companies
   - Check local tech companies, agencies, consultancies, SaaS companies, fintech, oil & gas tech subsidiaries, etc.

4. Re-check known careers pages for any new roles
5. Extract any software engineering roles found (past or present — capture the role, not just currently open)
6. For ALL discovered companies (with or without current openings), add to careers_registry.csv if new
7. Deduplicate against careers_registry.csv
8. Append new companies and any new jobs to their respective registries
9. Write careers/runs/run-YYYY-MM-DD.md
10. Commit and push meaningful changes to origin/main

Final reply must be brief:
- count of new companies discovered (even without current openings)
- count of new jobs found
